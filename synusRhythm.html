SINUS RHYTHM

  1. Home
  2. Learn
  3. Learning

Sinus rhythm refers to the normal electrical activity of the heart, originating from the sinoatrial (SA) node, which is the heart's natural pacemaker. In sinus rhythm, the electrical impulses follow a specific pathway, resulting in a regular heartbeat. This rhythm is considered the normal rhythm of a healthy heart.

Information

  • Heart Rate: 60 to 100 beats per minute
  • Rhythm: Regular
  • P wave: Present before each QRS complex
  • PR Interval: 0.12-0.20 seconds
  • QRS : Narrow and uniform in shape

Diagnosis

This ECG correspond to a healthy person.

Treatment

It is not necessary a treatment.
